Company

Mrs. R. Gokiladevi founded Naga Pasumai, a spice powder company, in 2017. With support from the government, the company expanded to include masala powders and now employs mostly women and differently abled persons from rural areas. Naga Pasumai's products are popular with housewives and restaurants for their easy and quick cooking solutions.
